Dear Mr. Doležal,

The course B0B04B22 - Anglický jazyk B2-2 is simply that – a voluntary
course to help prepare students for the FEL B2 English Exam should
they have to take it.

Should you pass the B0B04B22 - Anglický jazyk B2-2 course you will
receive 3 credits – as well as a very good idea of what is actually on
the FEL B2 English Exam and how to pass it.

If you need to sit the FEL B2 English Exam you must register for it as
its own separate subject (B0B04B2Z Anglický jazyk B2 - zkouška) –
which it is – and then you must sit the exam during the exam period
(for 0 credits).
A minimum of 50% is required to pass the FEL B2 English Exam.

Be sure that you have already fulfilled your other mandatory subject
B0B04B1K Anglický jazyk B1 - klasifikovaný zápočet.
If you have not, it could cause issues when attempting to register for
the B2 Exam subject B0B04B2Z Anglický jazyk B2 – zkouška.

ALL of this information (link to Moodle) would have been sent to you
in an email at the start of your studies.
